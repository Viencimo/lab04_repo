Problem Domain

There doesn’t really seem to be an app that’s tailored to schoolwork and questions that might stem from said school. Why isn’t there an app that:

  • acts as an agenda of sorts to keep track of work
  • allows the user to get help with questions from the comfort of their home
  • allows users to subscribe to a school ‘hub’ of sorts, where students post questions and get answers from their peers or tutors?

In addition

  • No other apps seem to group together questions and answers by schools or districts
  • No other apps seem to combine school features with an agenda for convenience
  • There’s quite the chance here!
